About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Informatics, University of Lugano, from September 2010. I am currently under the supervision of Prof. Walter Binder, working with Dr. Achille Peternier, Danilo Ansaloni, Lukáš Marek, and Aibek Sarimbekov, as a member of Dynamic Analysis Group. My research interests are workload characterization and performance prediction of consolidated virtual machines, concurrent and parallel pragramming, operating system and its security.
